How it works

Three steps to a fence
that lasts decades.

01

Hydraulic drive

Steel posts are driven deep into undisturbed soil using a hydraulic post driver. No digging. No concrete. No mess.

02

Soil compression

KC's clay-heavy soil grips the driven steel post through skin friction, creating a natural anchor stronger than any concrete footing.
