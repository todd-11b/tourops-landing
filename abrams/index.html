The Hidden Problem

Why Most Fences Fail

You've seen it in every neighborhood. A fence that looked great on day one — now leaning, rotting, falling apart.

It's not bad luck. It's bad installation.

Traditional fence posts are buried in concrete. Over time, that concrete traps moisture against the wood, causing posts to rot from the inside out. The very thing that's supposed to hold your fence up is what destroys it.

The problem isn't the fence. It's the post.

The SteelCore™ Difference

The SteelCore™ system drives steel fence posts deep into undisturbed soil — using the earth's natural compression instead of concrete.

KC's clay-heavy soil is actually an advantage. The dense, undisturbed ground grips the driven steel post through skin friction, creating a stronger natural anchor than any concrete footing.

  • Stronger structural support
  • Faster installation
  • No concrete rot
  • Cleaner installation — no mess
