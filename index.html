Common Questions

Everything you need to know before applying

Is it actually an AI, or a real person answering? +

It’s AI — built specifically for your tours, pricing, and FAQs. It answers calls, texts, chats, and DMs instantly, 24/7. If someone needs to be transferred to you live, it does that too. You hear everything. You’re always in control.

How fast can this be set up? +

Most operators are live within 48–72 hours. We handle the entire setup. You review it, approve it, and we go live. No tech skills required on your end.

Can I customize what it says? +

Yes. We train it on your tours, your pricing, your policies, and your brand voice. If something doesn’t sound right, we fix it. It’s built to sound like you — not like a generic bot.

What if a customer wants to talk to a real person? +

The AI can transfer calls live to you or anyone on your team. You set the rules — when to transfer, who to route to, and what happens after hours. Nothing slips through without a plan.

Will this work with my existing booking system? +

Yes. TourOps works alongside FareHarbor, Peek Pro, Xola, Rezdy, Bookeo, and most other booking platforms. We don’t replace your tools — we plug in around them so nothing changes for your customers.

What about follow-up after the first contact? +

Automated follow-up is built in. If someone doesn’t book after the first inquiry, TourOps follows up at the right intervals — by text or email — until they book or opt out. No leads fall through the cracks.

What if I’m not happy after go-live? +

If within 30 days of go-live it’s not a fit, I refund your onboarding fee. No questions asked.
